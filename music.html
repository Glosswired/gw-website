SiIvaGunner


I starting submitting high quality rips to SiIvaGunner in late 2019, and was invited to the team in 2020. I've created several rips ever since joining the team. Here are some of the works for the channel that I have on SoundCloud.

fusoxide · SiIvaGunner works

One of my favorite projects I've contributed to so far was the DJ Professor K radio stream, where they played several rips of Sega-related music. I had the opportunity to create two fake music tracks for the cancelled game, Jet Set Radio Evolution. The first track I created for this is "The Electrisco", an early 2000's inspired house track featuring lots of vocal samples. The other track was a funky big beat remix of "FM Funk" from the Sega arcade game Quartet; this remix also features an original guitar solo. I also created a regular short jingle rip for it too.
