Who am I?


I am fusoxide, a 19 year old person from Scotland. I am interested in many topics, such as retro computing, aesthetics, video games and music. My main hobbies are creating music, documenting obscure finds, and procrastinating.

My interests in more detail:

  • Gaming: Although I don't play games regularly nowadays, I have loved them since I was a kid. The ones I’m most into are probably by Nintendo, Sega and Namco. In particular, I enjoy video game music, especially obscure stuff.
  • 90s/2000s aesthetics: I dig aesthetics such as Y2K, cyber/Gen-X Corporate and Frutiger Aero, along with pre-2013 web aesthetics
  • Technology: I've always been into technology, especially the audiovisual aspects such as sounds, wallpapers and themes. I am also interested in tech-related history, such as of phones and Windows.
  • Anime: I only watch anime from time to time (and honestly mostly slice-of-life stuff), but I do love anime girls.

My hobbies in more detail:

  • Music composition/arrangement: Not an expert on music theory, but I love creating music, especially VGM-inspired stuff. You should check it out!
  • Design: I don’t really do graphic design as much as I used to anymore, but I still do this from time-to-time, mainly retro UI-related stuff.
  • Research/archival: I like to research and archive stuff related to my niche interests. Efforts I make include writing wiki pages, creating spreadsheets, contacting original authors, and dumping CDs.

Hopefully you share some of these interests! I would like to talk to more people with common interests. I also want to get into other media, so feel free to chat to me about basically anything in moderation!
